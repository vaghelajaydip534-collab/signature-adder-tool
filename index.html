Frequently Asked Questions

What formats can I download my signature in?

You can download your drawn signature as a transparent PNG. Typed signatures can be downloaded as both transparent PNG and SVG files.

Are the handwriting fonts free to use?

Yes, all the handwriting fonts used in this tool are from Google Fonts and are free for personal and commercial use.

Is my signature legally binding?

This tool is for creating a digital image of your signature. The legal validity of a digital signature depends on the legal jurisdiction and the context of its use. Please consult with a legal professional for advice on legally binding electronic signatures.

Is my signature data saved?

No, your signature is not saved on our servers. All processing is done in your browser, and your signature is deleted once you close the page.

I'm having trouble downloading my signature. What should I do?

Please ensure you are using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or contact us for support.
